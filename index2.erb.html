My Covid Panel

These graphs are built from data collected by the New York Times as well as from the CDC. The NYT code is generally 24 hours old. The CDC data about excess deaths is 1 week old, but with the important caveat that data newer than about 3 weeks is revised several times as time goes on so you can't really rely on what you see that is more recent than 3 weeks.

Excess deaths compare the number of reported deaths in the current period against the same period last year. The concept gives an interesting view of things without actually having to decide what the cause of death was.
